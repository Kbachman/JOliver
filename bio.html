Born in Whittier California in 1958. Grew up and went to school in both Southern and Northern California. Moved to Los Angeles in 1981 and exhibited paintings in various alternative venues. Moved to Seattle in 1989. Exhibited in various alternative venues and galleries, including Room 104, TPN Gallery Walls, The Kurt Lidtke Gallery, Collusion Unlimited, Solomon Fine Art, Artspace, The Sev Shoon Center, and The Battery Street Gallery.
